Advanced Fission (SMR)

NuScale, Rolls-Royce SMR, X-energy, TerraPower — next-gen nuclear that's smaller, safer, cheaper.

0 believers

  • NuScale has NRC design certification — first SMR approved in the US
  • Factory-built modules slash construction time from 10 years to 3
  • Passively safe designs — physically cannot melt down
  • 24/7 baseload power regardless of weather or time of day
  • TerraPower's Natrium plant backed by Bill Gates, under construction in Wyoming
My conclusion: Fission wins

Solar + Storage

Solar PV + lithium-ion / iron-air batteries — already the cheapest new electricity in most of the world.

0 believers

  • Solar LCOE dropped 90% in 15 years — already under $30/MWh in sunny regions
  • Battery costs falling 15% per year; 4-hour storage now economical
  • No fuel costs, no waste, no regulatory approval bottleneck
  • China installing 200+ GW of solar per year — unstoppable manufacturing scale
  • Form Energy's iron-air battery promises 100-hour storage at $20/kWh
My conclusion: Solar+Storage wins
