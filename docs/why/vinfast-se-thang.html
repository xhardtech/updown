Bang chung VinFast se thang

1. Xu huong EV toan cau khong the dao nguoc

EV da chiem 20%+ doanh so o to toan cau. Trung Quoc, chau Au, My deu dang chuyen doi. Viet Nam khong the di nguoc xu huong. VinFast la thuong hieu EV duy nhat san xuat tai VN.

2. Gia canh tranh — VF5 tu 500 trieu

VF5 Plus chi tu 500 trieu (thue pin), re hon Toyota Vios. VF6 tu 675 trieu canh tranh truc tiep Honda City. Khi gia EV = gia xe xang, khong ly do gi chon xe xang.

3. He sinh thai Vingroup — Loi the doc nhat

Khong hang xe nao co ecosystem nhu Vingroup: tram sac (40,000+ cong), Vinmec (bao hanh suc khoe), VinHomes (sac tai nha), VPBank (tai chinh). Mua VinFast = gia nhap he sinh thai.

4. Chi phi van hanh thap hon 3-4 lan

100km bang dien: ~20,000 VND. 100km bang xang: ~80,000-120,000 VND. Tiet kiem 5-8 trieu/thang neu chay nhieu. Chua tinh bao duong it hon (khong thay dau, loc...).

5. Chinh sach uu dai cua chinh phu

Mien thue truoc ba cho xe dien den 2027. Giam 50% phi dang ky. Nhieu tinh dang xay tram sac. Chinh phu VN da cam ket Net Zero 2050 — xe dien la bat buoc.

6. Tu hao dan toc — Yeu to cam xuc manh

VinFast la thuong hieu o to dau tien cua Viet Nam xuat khau ra the gioi. Mua VinFast = ung ho san pham Viet. Dieu nay tao dong luc mua manh me ma Toyota khong co.
