The case for VinFast winning

1. The global EV shift is irreversible

EVs already account for 20%+ of global car sales. China, Europe, and the US are all transitioning. Vietnam won't buck the trend. VinFast is the only EV brand manufacturing locally in Vietnam.

2. Competitive pricing — VF5 starting at 500M VND

The VF5 Plus starts at just 500 million VND (with battery lease), cheaper than a Toyota Vios. The VF6 at 675 million competes directly with the Honda City. When EV prices match gas car prices, there's no reason to choose gas.

3. The Vingroup ecosystem — an unmatched advantage

No other automaker has an ecosystem like Vingroup: charging stations (40,000+ ports), Vinmec (healthcare), VinHomes (home charging), VPBank (financing). Buying a VinFast means joining an entire ecosystem.

4. Operating costs 3-4x lower

100km on electricity: ~20,000 VND. 100km on gas: ~80,000-120,000 VND. That's 5-8 million VND saved per month for heavy drivers. And maintenance costs are far lower too — no oil changes, no filters.

5. Strong government incentives

Registration tax exemption for EVs through 2027. 50% reduction in registration fees. Multiple provinces are building charging infrastructure. Vietnam has committed to Net Zero by 2050 — EVs are mandatory.

6. National pride — a powerful emotional driver

VinFast is the first Vietnamese car brand exported worldwide. Buying VinFast means supporting a homegrown product. This creates a powerful purchase motivation that Toyota simply cannot match.
