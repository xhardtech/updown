Bang chung xe Nhat van thong tri

1. Do ben da duoc chung minh hang chuc nam

Toyota Vios chay 300,000km van on. Honda City 200,000km chua phai sua lon. VinFast moi co mat tu 2019 — chua ai biet xe VinFast chay 10 nam se nhu the nao.

2. Gia tri ban lai — Xe Nhat giu gia vuot troi

Toyota Vios sau 3 nam giu 75-80% gia tri. VinFast VF8 sau 1 nam mat 40-50%. Khi ban xe, nguoi mua xe Nhat lai duoc nhieu hon — do la tien that.

3. Mang luoi dai ly va bao duong rong khap

Toyota co 70+ dai ly tren toan quoc, den tan tinh le. Honda tuong tu. VinFast van dang xay dung — neu ban o nong thon, sua xe o dau?

4. Khong lo ve pin va ha tang sac

Thay pin VinFast: 200-400 trieu. Sac o chung cu? Rat kho. Di duong dai? Phai tinh toan ky. Xe xang: do xang 5 phut, tram xang khap noi, khong lo gi.

5. Hybrid — Giai phap trung gian hoan hao

Toyota Yaris Cross Hybrid, Honda City e:HEV — tiet kiem xang 50% ma khong can sac dien. Day co the la giai phap tot nhat cho VN trong 5-10 nam toi.

6. Thuc te thi truong — Xe Nhat van dan dau

Toyota Vios van la xe ban chay nhat VN nhieu nam lien. Thi phan xe Nhat van >50%. So lieu khong noi doi — nguoi dan VN van chon xe Nhat bang tien that.

Phe VinFast noi gi?

Xu huong EV toan cau

Dung, EV la tuong lai. Nhung "tuong lai" co the la 10-20 nam nua. Hien tai, ha tang VN chua san sang. Mua xe la quyet dinh hom nay, khong phai 2035.

Chi phi van hanh re hon

Dien re hon xang. Nhung khi tinh ca mat gia xe + chi phi thay pin, tong chi phi so huu (TCO) chua chac re hon. Can tinh ky, khong chi nhin tien dien.

Uu dai chinh phu

Mien thue truoc ba chi den 2027. Sau do? Se giong nhu moi xe khac. Uu dai tam thoi khong nen la ly do chinh de mua xe di 10 nam.
