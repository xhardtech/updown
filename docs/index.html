Digital Camera WON. Sasson was RIGHT.

Invented the digital camera at Kodak in 1975. Kodak forced him to hide it to protect film sales. 25 years later, Kodak filed for bankruptcy. His conviction score would have been 98/100 — maximum pressure, maximum vindication.

Nokia Engineers vs Management (2007 - 2013)

Touchscreen WON. Engineers were RIGHT.

Nokia engineers warned that touchscreen smartphones would dominate. Management doubled down on Symbian and physical keyboards. Nokia's market share dropped from 50% to 3%. Engineers who believed in touchscreen — while being forced to build Symbian — were vindicated.

Blockbuster employees who saw streaming as the future — while being told to sell DVD rentals. Netflix offered to sell to Blockbuster for $50M. Blockbuster laughed. Netflix is now worth $300B. Blockbuster is a single store in Oregon.
